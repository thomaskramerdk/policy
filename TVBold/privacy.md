# Privacy Policy for TV Bold

**Last Updated: January 10, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

TV Bold ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application TV Bold (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

TV Bold is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide you with match schedule information. Specifically:

### 2.1 Data Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including IP addresses, which are transmitted to our third-party service provider (Supabase) as part of normal network requests to fetch match schedule data.
- **App Usage Data:** The App locally stores usage count data (number of app launches) on your device using UserDefaults. This data is stored locally only and is not transmitted to us or any third party. This information is used solely to determine when to show app review prompts after 5 uses.
- **Network Connection Status:** The App monitors network connectivity status on your device to provide appropriate error messages. This monitoring occurs entirely on your device and is not transmitted externally.

### 2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

- Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, address)
- Location data
- Health or fitness data
- Financial information
- Contact information from your device
- Photos, videos, or other media from your device
- Browsing history or search history
- Authentication credentials or login information

## 3. How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

- **Automatically when you use the App:** When you open the App and it fetches match schedules, network requests are automatically made to Supabase, which may collect your device's IP address as part of standard network communication protocols.
- **Locally on your device:** App usage count is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **App Functionality:** To fetch and display football match schedules from our database service provider (Supabase).
- **Error Handling:** To handle network errors and provide appropriate feedback when the App cannot connect to our services.
- **App Review Prompts:** To track app usage locally on your device to determine when to show Apple's native app review prompt (after 5 uses). This tracking is done entirely on your device and is not shared with us.

## 5. Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

### 5.1 Supabase

We use Supabase (supabase-swift SDK) as our backend database service to store and retrieve football match schedule data. Supabase may collect:

- IP addresses of devices making requests to their servers
- Device identifiers for connection management
- Error logs for debugging purposes (if error reporting is enabled)

Supabase uses this information to provide database services to us. Supabase's collection and use of information is governed by their own Privacy Policy, available at: https://supabase.com/privacy

We confirm that Supabase provides the same or equal protection of user data as stated in this Privacy Policy. Data collected by Supabase is used solely for app functionality and is not used for tracking, advertising, or marketing purposes.

### 5.2 Firebase (Google)

The App includes Firebase SDK with Firebase Analytics enabled. Firebase Analytics collects usage data and events to help us understand how the App is used and improve user experience. Firebase may collect:
- Device information (device model, OS version, device identifiers)
- App usage events and interactions
- Performance data and crash reports
- IP addresses (as part of standard network requests)

This data is collected automatically and is used for analytics purposes only. If you would like more information about Firebase's data practices, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 5.3 Google Mobile Ads

The App includes Google Mobile Ads SDK in the codebase for potential future use. Currently, Google Mobile Ads is **deactivated** and not initialized in the App. The SDK does not collect any data as it is not actively running. If Google Mobile Ads is enabled in the future, its data practices will be governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 6. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

- All network communications between the App and our servers are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.
- Local data stored on your device (app usage count) is stored in iOS's secure UserDefaults storage, which is sandboxed and protected by iOS's security mechanisms.
- We do not maintain our own servers or databases. All match schedule data is stored and managed by Supabase, which employs industry-standard security measures.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

### 7.1 Data Retention

- **Supabase Data:** IP addresses and device identifiers collected by Supabase are retained according to Supabase's data retention policies. Typically, this data is retained only as long as necessary for providing the service or as required by law.
- **Local Device Data:** App usage count stored locally on your device persists until you uninstall the App or clear the App's data.

### 7.2 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data in the following ways:

- **Local Data:** To delete locally stored app usage data, you can uninstall the App from your device. This will remove all local data stored by the App.
- **Supabase Data:** To request deletion of data collected by Supabase (such as IP addresses), please contact us using the contact information provided at the end of this Privacy Policy. We will work with Supabase to facilitate the deletion of any data associated with your device.

Please note that we may retain certain information if required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as resolving disputes or enforcing our agreements.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

- **Access:** You can review what data is stored locally by checking your device settings. For data collected by third parties, please contact us for more information.
- **Deletion:** You can delete locally stored data by uninstalling the App. You can request deletion of third-party collected data by contacting us.
- **Withdraw Consent:** If you do not wish to have your IP address collected by Supabase, you can stop using the App. Unfortunately, the App requires network connectivity to function, and IP addresses are automatically collected as part of standard network protocols.
- **App Tracking Transparency:** If the App implements tracking features in the future, you will have the ability to control tracking through iOS's App Tracking Transparency settings.

## 9. Children's Privacy
